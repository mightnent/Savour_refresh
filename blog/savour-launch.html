Join Us For Savour!’s Official Platform Launch Event on 15th August 2020


Savour! is officially launching our platform soon and we are celebrating our launch with our official virtual platform launch event happening on 15th August 2020, Saturday, 10am to 12pm SGT, on Zoom.

Join us by RSVP-ing in this form below now by 9 August 2020 and we will email you the Zoom meeting link upon registration:
http://tinyurl.com/savourlaunch

Our programme highlights are as follows:

  • Speech by our co-founders and guest of honors
  • Unveiling of our platform
  • Participate in a quiz where you can stand a chance to win attractive prizes
  • Play our special launch video
  • Share our fundraising initiative to support nonprofits
  • Announce our special launch promotions
  • Kickstart our social media contest.
  • Our programme hosts conducting a series of live virtual workshops to demonstrate how to utilise expiring, blemished and surplus food ingredients to convert into other useful products

We look forward to seeing you there! From all of us in the Savour! Team, we will like to thank you for your support!
