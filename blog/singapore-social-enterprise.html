The Ultimate Guide of 52 F&B Social Enterprises in Singapore


While Singapore is home to notable unicorn tech startups, it is also a place where social enterprises are alive and well. According to the ranking by ACRA, Singapore ranks third among 190 economies in the “Starting a Business” indicator, and this is evident in the increasing number of startups across all industries, emerging now and then. As we work towards becoming a more progressive nation by reducing the social inequality in our society, many businesses have reinvented new ways of doing businesses by having an element of social purpose to it. 

We have compiled 52 F&B social enterprises in Singapore and categorised them into bakeries, cafes, restaurants, snack and dessert shops, and grocery wholesalers and retailers. Do give them some support by making a purchase from them -- and you will get to eat food, do good, and feel good -- all at once. Isn’t that great?
