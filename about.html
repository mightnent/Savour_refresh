How We Started

Savour! is a tech social enterprise started in 2019 by a group of NUS and SUTD undergraduates who are passionate about saving the environment and helping those in need. Savour! started off initially as an app to reduce food wastage by listing expiring/blemished/surplus food for sale to the general public. We then expanded our platform offerings to encompass more types of products. We constantly evolve and adapt to better serve our partners' needs.
