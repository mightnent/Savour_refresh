Savour! Payment Policy


  1. While we do our best to ensure that you have a good experience procuring products and services from our platform, we want to sort out any issues for you. You may contact SAVOUR! through email at hello@savouapp.co in the event of:
    1. Wrong products: If the products you received are of wrong quantity, size, color or design, please take a photo and provide a short description. Due to differences in lighting or production batches, light hue or saturation discrepancies may be present. Hence, minimal variations of color cannot be totally avoided.
    2. Damaged products: If the products you received are found to have damages or defects, please take a photo and provide a short description. Compensation for partial damage products is subjected to the severity of damage.
    3. Missing products: It is imperative to check the quantity of your items as soon as possible upon self-collection or delivery. Upon self-collection or delivery, if you notice that the products you received are less in quantity then what you made payment for, please raise this issue to the staff at the outlet or the deliveryman and take a photo with the order of your products and provide a short description. Compensation will be determined on a case-by-case basis after checking with our team and merchants.
    4. Unsatisfactory services: If the services rendered to you do not meet the requirements as stated on SAVOUR! when you made payment, please provide evidence in the form of photos or videos of the service as well as a short description. Compensation will be determined on a case-by-case basis after checking with our team and merchants.
  2. All issues have to be raised to us within 7 days from the date of receipt by the customer. We will then verify the issue on a case-by-case basis and get back to you within 3-5 working days after checking with our team and merchants. If the email correspondence is inactive beyond 7 days, our team may close the request accordingly.
  3. Please note that this warranty is void if the product is misused, altered, mishandled, improperly used or due to negligence.
