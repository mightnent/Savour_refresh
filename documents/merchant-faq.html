Merchant FAQ

How is SAVOUR! an e-Procurement platform?

SAVOUR! assists your business in generating more leads on our business facing website. Business clients can directly buy from you on our platform.

What is the pricing/fee structure on SAVOUR!

SAVOUR!’s platform is free to sign up and use. We do charge a small service fee on a per transaction basis. You can find out more at https://www.savourapp.co/documents/fee-policy

How can I sell to my existing buyers through SAVOUR!?

If you have an existing buyer, no worries - all we have to do is onboard them for you. Having them on our platform gives you visibility on your earnings with them. You can simply email us at hello@savourapp.co to find out more.

Can I monitor my orders and performance on Savour?

We have an inbuilt dashboard for merchants in SAVOUR! platform that gives you order information and transaction overview at one glance. You can also easily generate printable PDFs’ for past transactions for filing purposes.

Can I go on a trial period before fully committing?

Yes you can! We are always open to letting businesses try out our platform and see if we are the right fit for them. Feel free to email us at hello@savourapp.co to let us know of your interest to go on a trial period and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible!

I am a supplier/brand owner and would like to supply my products on SAVOUR!. How do I start the conversation?

If you are interested to partner with us, do email us at hello@savourapp.co regarding what products you would like to list on our website and we'll get back to you as soon as possible!

I am looking to collaborate or enter into a partnership with SAVOUR!, who can I reach out to in regards to this?

We are constantly looking for ways to partner with the right businesses. Please feel free to email us at hello@savourapp.co regarding how you would like to partner us and we'll get back to you as soon as possible!
